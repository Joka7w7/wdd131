Site Plan: All-In-One Goods

Site Name

All-In-One Goods – This name represents a central hub for a small business that sells a variety of products. It reflects convenience and versatility in one brand. This name is clear, marketable, and future-proof.

Optional domain availability: allinonegoods.com (subject to availability)

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to showcase a variety of product categories in a simple, clean way that invites users to browse, favorite items, and place orders or ask for more information. This website acts as a stepping stone toward launching a real e-commerce store in the future and serves as a portfolio project demonstrating practical web development skills.

Scenarios

  • What types of products are currently available for sale?
  • How can I save items to my favorites or shopping list for later?
  • Is there a way to contact the seller to ask questions before ordering?

Color Schema

The selected color scheme reflects trust, clarity, and a clean shopping experience:

  • Primary Green (#2a7f62) – used for headings, buttons, and accents.
  • Light Gray (#f8f8f8) – used for background and containers.

Typography

  • Roboto Slab – used for main headings and titles to add a touch of elegance.
  • Arial – used for body text to maintain readability across devices.
